About currencies

Currencies are the accepted forms of money used within a country or economic region for transactions and trade. Each country typically has its own currency, which is controlled by its central bank or monetary authority. Here is a brief overview of some of the major world currencies:

Country Capital Currency
United States Washington, D.C. US Dollar (USD)
India New Delhi Indian Rupee (INR)
Japan Tokyo Japanese Yen (JPY)
Germany Berlin Euro (EUR)
Australia Canberra Australian Dollar (AUD)
